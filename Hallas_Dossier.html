I. Identity & Standing

Hallas is a Ranger of the North, paired with Damrod. He and Damrod arrived in the snowfields above the Frost Maw at the moment of Celenneth and Linnea's blizzard, not as rescuers but as equals with their own mission. Hallas was the one who recognised Celenneth as Brandor and Orlec's protégé — the detail that made the alliance feel real rather than convenient. He fought beside the family through the descent, supplied corrections from older knowledge during the rune restoration, and rode east at the parting with Damrod. After his return to the road east, Celenneth named her son for him.
