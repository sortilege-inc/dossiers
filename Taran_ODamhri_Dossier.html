Artillerist — Eldritch Cannon
Flamethrower · Explosive Cannon
Each creature in a 15-ft. cone must succeed on a DC 14 Dex save or take 2d8 fire damage (half on success). Flammable objects in the area that aren't being worn or carried ignite.

Explosive Cannon (9th lvl): As an action, destroy the cannon — it explodes in a 20-ft. radius around it. Each creature in that area must succeed on a DC 14 Dex save or take 3d8 force damage (half on success).

The cannon is a Tiny object, AC 18, HP 45 (5 × artificer level). It can be moved up to 15 ft. as a bonus action. Lasts 1 hour or until destroyed. Created as an action; can only have one at a time (or two at 15th level). Cannon shares the caster's spell attack modifier.

⚙Character⚙
Personality
Clever and unsentimental. Loves family but has long been eager to roam. Mercenary artillerist work has been his means of seeing the world.
Ideals
A pragmatist. Little use for moralising. Will leave others to their business so long as they leave him to his. There are some lines he will not cross.
Bonds
Pietro — the wooden monkey homunculus — is considered almost a pet. A rare attachment for an otherwise unsentimental dwarf.
Flaws
Brusque. Explains himself once at most. Others can keep up or not — their problem, not his.
