Xemnon Sunguides

Stage of Career

Bioα · Vita

Xemnon is the epitome of physical and mental fortitude, with a lionhearted courage and resilience. He sees humans as naïve to think the gods are looking out for them — yet he envies that innocence. He stays up late sometimes, watching the stars, hoping Heliod will speak to him again.

His one and only moment of communion came after he rescued a pegasus trapped in a net. Heliod spoke, commending his courage and respect for the sacred creature, and told Xemnon to be a protector of others. That moment confirmed his piety at score 3 and granted him his Devotee trait.

He performed his mandatory militia service with the Sunguides pride when he came of age, holding the rank of Sergeant. With service complete, he is free now to explore the world — and wants, above all, to be renowned among the leonin.
