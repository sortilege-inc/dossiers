Chrythra Thanessa

Golden light at the end of life, softened death — a Gorgon herbalist who attends births and deaths with equal interest, and is not convinced this fallow season should be averted.

Stage of Career

Bioα · Vita

Chrythra — from chryso, golden, the light at the end of life. Thanessa — from Thanatos, death, softened to convey care. She is more than a century old, lives in the Nessian Wood with her serpents and her cards, and is the only one in the party who can remember the last fallow season. She is not convinced the self-destruction of society is the wrong outcome.

Her teacher was a serpent — sagacious, multilingual, and (Chrythra suspects, though she has never asked) a manifestation of Pharika herself. Through it she learned alchemy, poisons, cures, and the soothsayer's craft. She moves between the wood and the world, attending births and deaths with equal interest, and keeps a polished obsidian-and-bone deck whose every reading lands more accurately than it should.

Having the right answers is the first step to solving any problem, no matter how dire. That is what she tells herself.

Why Chrythra is on the Expeditionο · Hodós

Shadows of the Past. Chrythra is the only one who remembers the last fallow season, but she isn't convinced that the self-destruction of society is the wrong outcome. She is more than a century old; she watched the gods turn away once before, and watched what mortals did with the silence.

She is on this expedition not because she wants to save the world, but because she wants to see — closely, this time — what happens when it ends. Pharika is patient with her. The serpents are patient with her. The cards are patient with her. The Fallow Year is the cycle Chrythra has waited a long time to read.
