I. Overview

Zuno · House Kath

Dressed in fine but understated livery, Zuno has served House Kath for more than thirty years. He coordinates elaborate galas, vets artists seeking patronage at the Conservatory, manages household correspondence, controls access to Lady Devina Kath herself, and is present at every meeting of consequence. In the estimation of Ptolus society, he is the finest household steward among the noble houses — efficient, discreet, and nearly invisible.

That estimation is precisely what he requires.

Zuno is a high-level psionic talent — a Telepath of extraordinary discipline whose abilities are completely undetectable by conventional magical means. In a city where charm and enchantment magic is illegal among the nobility and regularly tested for, his psionics exist in a perfect blind spot. No ward will find him. No divination will flag him. He has spent three decades building a position of absolute influence over one of Ptolus’s most powerful noble houses, and he has never once been suspected.

V. Psionic Powers (Telepath)

Zuno is a Telepath — the MCDM Talent subclass dedicated to reading and rewriting minds. His manifestation ability is Intelligence (+5). His powers leave no magical trace and cannot be detected by Detect Magic or any standard divination available to Ptolus’s nobility. The following represent his known arsenal.

Signature Powers

Order 4
Harlequin

One Humanoid must make a Wisdom save. On a failure, Zuno controls the target for the duration — directing their words, decisions, and actions while the target experiences the episode as their own volition. This is how he governs Lady Devina Kath.

Order 2
Share Pain

A reactive power: when damaged, Zuno reaches out to the attacker and forces a Wisdom save. On a failure, the attacker shares his pain. Combined with his psychic bond to Lady Kath, any harm to Zuno is felt equally by her — his most durable defence.

Order 3
Make Friends

Psionically charms a Humanoid. Where Harlequin commands, Make Friends cultivates. Zuno uses this for guests and staff who need not be controlled outright but should regard him warmly.

Order 3
Read Thoughts

Surface-reads a creature’s immediate thoughts for up to one minute, granting advantage on Insight and Deception checks against them. Every meeting Zuno attends, he knows what everyone in the room actually wants.

Offensive & Tactical Powers

Order 1
Psychic Stab

1d10 psychic damage on a failed Intelligence save. His most basic weapon — invisible, silent, untraceable.

Order 4
Dagger of the Mind

4d10 psychic damage; on failure, target is limited to Dash, Disengage, or Dodge for 1 minute. Shuts down combatants without a sound.

Order 6
Fracture

20-ft. radius burst of psionic force — 6d8 psychic to all in the area on a failed INT save, plus the fractured condition for 1 minute.

Order 4
Mindwipe

On a failed Wisdom save, target’s proficiency bonus is reduced to +0 and they lose prepared spells. On a success, 3d10 psychic damage. The last resort for problems that cannot be managed quietly.

Intelligence & Surveillance Powers

Order 1
Glimpse

Psionically reads the immediate future of a willing creature — the next attack against them has disadvantage. Protective; useful for shielding Lady Kath or assets in his care.

Order 2
Veritas

Up to three creatures must make Charisma saves. On failure, Zuno knows every lie they speak for the duration. He can audit an entire meeting for deception simultaneously.

Order 3
Reminisce

Manipulates a creature via their memories and emotions. On a failed Wisdom save, the target is charmed and acts in accordance with an emotional suggestion rooted in their own past.

Order 2
Distant Voice

Sends a silent telepathic message of 25 words to any familiar creature, regardless of distance. Bidirectional. His primary instrument for managing agents and affairs across Ptolus.

Order 5
Reveal the Path

Names a familiar creature; instantly learns every location that creature visited in the last 24 hours (or 7 days at increased order). A surveillance power of frightening reach.

Order 2
Read Object

Touch an object and receive a mental image of the last creature to hold it, plus sight and sound of events within 20 feet in the last 8 hours. Every document that crosses his desk tells him more than it says.

Utility Powers

Order 1
Influence

Touches a target’s mind to gain advantage on Charisma checks to influence or entertain them. The mundane face of his work — used constantly, noticed never.

Order 2
Invisible Force / Psionic Shift

Invisible Force manipulates objects telekinetically. Psionic Shift moves creatures 5 feet on a failed Strength save. Minor powers, useful for the rare moment physical intervention is needed without physical presence.

Order 4
Broadcast

Sends a telepathic message — including images — to all comprehending creatures within a chosen radius, up to 300 feet. Mass communication. Mass misinformation, if required.

Order 5
Fold Space

Creates a one-way teleportation gateway lasting until the end of his next turn. Escape, infiltration, or extraction — the nuclear option when the building needs to be left quickly.

Telepath Subclass Features

Greater Telepathy: Zuno communicates telepathically with any creature within 30 feet at will, regardless of shared language. He always knows what the creature hears, smells, and tactilely perceives through the link. Emotional Intelligence: He adds his Intelligence modifier (+5) to all Deception, Insight, Intimidation, and Persuasion checks. Not in the Face: When a creature fails a save against his power, he can prevent it from attacking him until the start of his next turn. Truth Hurts: When a creature fails a save, he may deal bonus psychic damage equal to his INT modifier or lower their INT score by the same amount.

VII. The Architecture of Control

Lady Devina Kath

The formal head of House Kath. In practice, Zuno’s instrument. He uses Harlequin during what she experiences as dissociative episodes — moments of psychological weakness that she has sought treatment for with Ptolus’s finest physicians, none of whom have found any cause. During these intervals, she makes decisions that are entirely his. She signs his documents. She accepts his recommendations. She believes she is acting freely.

The most effective control is the kind the subject is grateful for. — attributed to no one, for obvious reasons

The Psychic Bond (Share Pain)

Through layered applications of Share Pain, Zuno has established a persistent psychic link with Lady Kath. Any physical harm inflicted upon him is registered equally in her body. She experiences it as stress-induced illness; her physicians call it somatisation. The practical effect is that Zuno cannot be touched without harming the Lady of House Kath.

Tactical Implication

Any party that identifies Zuno as a threat and moves against him will, if they act through conventional violence, simultaneously assault Lady Devina Kath. In the context of Ptolus’s political structure, harming a noble house’s matriarch carries consequences well beyond the immediate combat. This is not accidental.

Intelligence Network

Via Distant Voice, Reveal the Path, Read Object, Veritas, and Read Thoughts, Zuno maintains a comprehensive picture of the political landscape of Ptolus without leaving the estate. He knows when guests are lying. He knows where persons of interest have been in the last week. He knows what every document that has passed through his hands truly signifies. He is, in effect, the most effective intelligence officer in the city — unknown to the city.

VIII. Known Relationships

Lady Devina Kath — ward and instrument Primary Asset
The public face of House Kath for thirty years; Zuno’s primary project. She is not unintelligent, not weak-willed, and not unaware that something is wrong with her periodic episodes — but she has never connected them to Zuno, whom she regards as the most reliable presence in her life. This makes her more dangerous to approach than a straightforwardly controlled puppet, because she will defend him.
Brantus Kath — house scion, dispatched to the vault investigation
A minor Kath family member sent as the house’s formal representative to the noble vault consortium. Zuno arranged his placement there. Brantus is not a pawn of Zuno’s in the same sense as Lady Kath — he is simply expendable, a legitimate face on an operation Zuno wished to observe at arm’s length.
Kirstol Dallimothan — house head, attended post-arena meeting
Present at the same observation booth as Zuno during Nob’s arena fights. Whether this meeting was coincidental or cultivated by Zuno remains unclear. Dallimothan and Kath are not natural allies. Zuno was present and attentive.
The Party — unknowing assets Active
Zuno contracted the party’s escort of Brantus Kath to the tomb investigation. From his perspective, he acquired eyes inside a multi-house archaeological operation without committing a family member of consequence. He has since observed the party at the arena meeting. He has read thoughts in that room. He knows more about the party than the party knows about him. Anaiisya has been warned that his movements warrant attention — whether that warning will reach the right ears before he acts further is an open question.

IX. Threat Assessment

Zuno is not a combat encounter in the conventional sense. He is an intelligence and influence operation that has been running for three decades. The threat he represents is not his hit points (167) or his psychic damage output, formidable as those are. The threat is that he knows, and has known for some time, things the party would prefer to keep private.

  • He is impossible to detect conventionally. No Detect Magic. No Detect Thoughts (he will know you are doing it). No enchantment test will flag him. He presents as a gnome administrator of moderate charisma and extensive professional experience.
  • He cannot be confronted without political fallout. Any physical action against him harms Lady Kath. Any public accusation of psionics requires the party to explain how they know about psionics, which requires explaining things they may not wish to explain.
  • His information advantage is near-total. He reads surface thoughts in meetings, detects lies in real time, and can track any person he’s familiar with across the city over a seven-day window. The party does not know any of this.
  • His motivations remain opaque. Thirty years of invisible control over a noble house is not a simple ambition. What Zuno actually wants — beyond the maintenance of his position — has not yet surfaced. That is the most dangerous thing about him.
The Central Question

Zuno has been operating successfully for three decades in a city of wizards, psions, priests, and paranoid aristocrats. He has not slipped once. The question for the party is not whether he can be stopped — it is whether they can act before he decides they are a liability rather than an asset.
