Bioα · Vita

Alesta's mother is an oread, and Alesta herself is Nyxborn — a fragment of divinity wearing a human shape, born on the spring equinox at dawn and dedicated to Nylea on the same morning. Her laughter causes birds to erupt in song. She has tremendous empathy for all who suffer, and a knack for healing things that are broken. She is currently undergoing a pilgrimage — a sacred hunt, not to kill but to speak with a golden stag.

She has lived in the Lagonna borderlands long enough to be called Centaur-Speaker. She is on the road as a young druid because the world is changing in ways the wood can feel before the cities can, and her own gods feel further away than they once did. Free thinking, she believes, is how we progress: inquiry and curiosity. She still seeks the enlightenment she sought in seclusion, and it still eludes her.

Feat: Fey-Touched

  • Ability Score Increase: +1 Wis (already applied).
  • Learn Misty Step and one 1st-level Divination or Enchantment spell — chosen: Beast Bond (1st-level Divination on the Druid spell list — fits Alesta perfectly as the Centaur-Speaker).
  • Cast each spell once without expending a spell slot. Regain on a long rest. Can also cast either spell using a spell slot of the appropriate level.
  • Spellcasting ability: Wis (since both are also on the Druid spell list).

Fey-Touched is a strong utility pick: Misty Step alone is a defensive teleport that helps Alesta's low AC/HP, and Beast Bond fits her "Centaur-Speaker" flavor while granting a beast advantage on attacks within 5 ft of her.

Supernatural Gift — Nyxbornθ · Dóron

Origin #1 — "One of my parents is a nyxborn, a nymph, or some other supernatural creature." Alesta's mother is an oread nymph.

  • Cloak of Stars: As an action, you wrap yourself in a starry cloak, appearing like a silhouette formed of the night sky. While the cloak is up, attack rolls have disadvantage against you. Lasts 1 minute, until you doff it (no action required), or until you die. 1/long rest.
  • Nyxborn Resistance: Resistance to necrotic AND radiant damage.

Cloak of Stars is Alesta's emergency defense — a get-out-of-frontline button that turns her squishy AC 12 into a much more survivable target for an entire minute. Pair with Misty Step to disengage from melee, or with Pass Without Trace once she has it at L3. Nyxborn Resistance covers two damage types common in divine-themed Theros encounters.

Why Alesta is on the Expeditionο · Hodós

Cultural Mediation. Her role as a centaur ambassador could force her to broker peace between groups fighting over dwindling resources. The Fallow Year stresses the Lagonna borderlands harder than most places — centaur tribes need land, satyrs need land, the human poleis push outward.

A Centaur-Speaker who also speaks human, leonin, and Sylvan, who has Nylea's favor and a nymph mother, is exactly the person each side wants to talk to and each side suspects of being secretly aligned with another. Alesta walks the pilgrimage of the golden stag partly because it's a holy quest and partly because being on the move is the only way to stay out of any one camp's claim on her loyalty.
