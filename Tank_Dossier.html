History

Toya had attended the mutant pride march in Tallahassee and tried to go incognito, but when things started getting rough they stepped up to protect the marchers when the police wouldn't.

Having faced the seeming failure of protesting and petitioning, Toya has used that rejection to energize their commitment to direct action, having formed X-FRONT with Tabitha.

Psych Profile

Personality
As the eldest and co-founder of X-FRONT, Toya has a presumption to authority and resists accountability.
Goal
Wants the freedom to act with the power of the group behind them, but chafes at being told what to do.
Barrier
Given the volatile combination of the members of X-FRONT, leadership requires self-control, confidence, and clarity. Ambiguity and recklessness lead to pulling in different directions, impeding progress, and frustrations.

Battle Plan
Standard · 1 Round · Cost: 10 Focus
The character inspires one or more allies of their choice in earshot, up to the character's Vigilance. Inspired allies gain an edge on all action checks until the start of the character's next turn.
Focus Fire
Standard · Concentration · Cost: 10 Focus
The character calls out an enemy in line of sight and inspires one or more allies of their choice in earshot, up to the character's Vigilance. They gain an edge on all action checks against that enemy.
Keep Moving
Reaction · Instant
Trigger: An ally in line of sight and earshot is demoralized or stunned. The demoralized or stunned condition ends.
On Your Feet
Reaction · Instant · Cost: 5 Focus
Trigger: An ally in line of sight and earshot is knocked prone. All prone allies within earshot, who are able to, can immediately stand up for free. Allies currently unable to stand up for any reason are not affected.
Rally On Me
Standard · Instant · Cost: 10 Focus · Once Per Battle
Any allies in earshot can be affected, up to a number equal to the character's Vigilance. Each affected ally can move toward the character at half speed. If they are within the character's reach at the end of this move, they recover lost Focus equal to 5 times the character's rank.

Traits & Tags

Abrasive

The character rubs people the wrong way. This gives them trouble when trying to make Ego checks to persuade someone to help them out. It gives them an edge when they're trying to make Ego checks to intimidate someone.

Audience

Many people follow the character's work and treat them with the respect they deserve. By making an Ego check, the character can persuade their audience to provide help in the form of information or resources. The Narrator determines the TN of the Ego check based on the favor requested.

Connections (Community)

By making an Ego check, the character can call on their contact to provide help in the form of clues, information or resources. The Narrator determines the TN of the Ego check based on the favor requested.

Fearless

The character is extremely brave. They have an edge on any action checks required to deal with fear.

Font of Information

The character has an edge on Logic checks having to do with knowledge.

Fresh Eyes

The character has their own way of doing things. This often makes them seem strange to those around them, but it means they can bring new perspectives to existing issues. They have an edge on Logic checks when faced with something for the first time.

Presence

The character knows how to command attention on demand. They have an edge on Ego checks that involve getting people to pay attention to them or to voluntarily do things for them.

Hounded

The character is a member of a group of people who are persecuted for simply being themselves.

X-Gene

The character has mutant genes that cause them to develop super-powers.
