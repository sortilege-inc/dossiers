Selene Shadowfoot

All wit and silver tongue — always one step from the big score, always one step ahead of the wrong person she's already fleeced.

Stage of Career

Bioα · Vita

Selene is clever and crafty, all wit and silver tongue — always one step from the big score that will change her fortune for good. She pulled off a daring heist as a young satyr, and that act caught Phenax's attention; the god of deception has watched her with interest ever since.

She is rarely without Lyra Hindparts, a bard with whom she shares a lifelong companion bond. The two are inseparable — always comrades, and occasionally partners in crime. Where Lyra performs and persuades, Selene listens, lifts purses, and slips out before anyone has noticed what just happened.

She struggles with trust. She has, by her own admission, fleeced the wrong person once — and now needs to make sure they don't come after her, or after Lyra.

Why Selene is on the Expeditionο · Hodós

The Price of Information. The Fallow Year is, fundamentally, a problem for poleis — and poleis are Selene's hunting grounds. A famine creates opportunity (desperate marks, panicked merchants, lax civic order) and risk (Akros's increased patrols, Meletis's tightened harbor security). Phenax would expect his devotees to thrive in such conditions.

Per the campaign doc plot hooks, Selene's information network may prove crucial for navigating divided allegiances during the crisis — she has eyes in places others can't reach, and the price of her information has rarely been higher.
