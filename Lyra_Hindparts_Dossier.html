Bioα · Vita

Suffused with charisma and optimism, Lyra is open and genuine — and remarkably cagey about certain topics. She's easily distracted, loves pranks and games of wits, and seems to know a story relevant to every occasion. She grew up in the Dakra Isles, where she met Erytheia. Somewhere along the way she crossed paths with Selene Shadowfoot, the satyr rogue, and the two of them have been bonded since — Lifelong Companions in the formal sense, partners in mischief in the everyday one. Where Selene listens and lifts, Lyra performs and persuades. When the trick comes off, there's usually a song about it within the week.

The dreams started a few years ago — strange ones, ones she couldn't shake. She consulted an oracle of Kruphix. Since then, the dreams have only gotten stranger. Mysteries find her now. She thinks the cosmos is more beautiful for keeping its greatest truths unknown, and tries to live by that, even when curiosity itches her like a fly bite.

Why Lyra is on the Expeditionο · Hodós

Secrets of Song. Per the campaign doc plot hook: Lyra learns some of the mysteries hidden by Karametra in nursery rhymes and songs that contain essential wisdom for surviving the Fallow season. Kruphix would smile on that work — a god of secrets sending a bard out to recover a god of harvest's hidden lessons, sung into children's lullabies because that was the only safe place to put them.

Lyra has the ear for it, the memory for it, and the disposition — old songs catch in her the way they catch in nobody else.
