History

The recent law passed in Florida about state-sanctioned kidnapping of mutant minors was controversial but seemingly theoretical. When a high-school basketball game was interrupted by one of the cheering students seemingly erupting in light & knocking out the power for three blocks, an investigation occurred, and state officials came to the home of Taylor Crenshaw and her parents begrudgingly handed her over despite protests. It garnered national media coverage.

Psych Profile

Personality
Energetic, impatient, tactless. Very ADHD, very inexperienced.
Goal
Taylor sees her power as something to be celebrated, not hidden, and wants to do good with it.
Barrier
Taylor knows that being able to remain with her family and friends means pretending that she isn't a mutant.

◈ Elemental Control (Electricity/Energy)
Elemental Burst
Standard · Instant · Range: 10 spaces
The character makes a ranged attack against an enemy in line of sight. If the attack is a success, it inflicts regular damage. On a Fantastic success, the enemy takes double damage instead and is stunned for one round.
Elemental Blast
Standard · Instant · Range: 10 spaces · Cost: 5+ Focus
The character makes a ranged attack with an edge at an enemy in line of sight. For this attack, add +1 to the character's Agility damage bonus for every 2 points of Focus they spend. On a success, an affected target takes that total damage. On a Fantastic success, an affected target takes double that total damage and is stunned for one round.
Elemental Barrage
Standard · Instant · Cost: 15 Focus · Electricity only
The character designates a space within their line of sight. The attack can affect every enemy within 10 spaces of that. The character makes a single Ego check and compares it to each target's Resilience defense. Affected enemies take half regular damage. On a Fantastic success, they take full regular damage and are stunned for one round.
Elemental Push
Standard · Instant · Cost: 10 Focus
The character makes an Ego attack against the target's Agility defense. If the attack succeeds, the character can move the target in any direction, up to 1 space times the character's rank. On a Fantastic success, the target also takes regular damage, is knocked prone and is stunned for one round.
Elemental Ricochet
Standard · Instant · Range: 40 spaces · Cost: 10 Focus · Electricity only
The character makes a ranged attack against a target in line of sight. If the attack is a success, it inflicts regular damage. On a Fantastic success, energy also bounces off the first target to another in line of sight, and the character can make a new attack against that target, adding the new range to the previous attack's range. This can be repeated until an attack is not fantastic.
Energy Absorption
Reaction · Instant · Cost: 15 Focus
Trigger: The character takes Health damage. The character can take any Health damage done to them (after applying any damage reduction), ignore it and add that number to their Focus instead. In this way, they can increase their Focus up to double their regular maximum Focus. Once the combat is over, any extra Focus over the character's regular maximum Focus score fades away. This power cannot be used again until any Focus the character gained in this way is spent.
◈ Signature Attack
Supernova
Standard · Instant · Cost: 15+ Focus
The character makes an Ego check and compares that against the Resilience defense of every enemy within 10 spaces. For these attacks, add +1 to the character's Ego damage bonus for every 2 points of Focus they spend. On a success, an affected target takes half that total damage. On a Fantastic success, an affected target takes full damage and is stunned for one round.

Traits & Tags

Famous

The character is widely known—at least among a certain group of people or a population. They may be well-liked or they may be hated, but either way, they are famous. They have an edge when making an Ego check to persuade someone who thinks favorably of them. They have trouble when making an Ego check to persuade someone who dislikes them.

Gullible

The character is easy to fool. People lying to them have an edge on their Ego checks to persuade the character of something.

Honest

The character is a terrible liar. They have trouble any time they make an Ego check that involves telling a lie. However, they have an edge on any friendly Ego (persuasion) checks in which they're truthful.

Inventor

The character is good at coming up with solutions on the fly. They have an edge on Logic checks when creating or repairing things.

Quick Learner

If the character fails an action check, they gain an edge on the check if they try the same action again on their next turn.

Signature Attack (Supernova)

The character is known for favoring a particular kind of weapon or attack power. They have an edge when making attacks that way.

Small

The character's size is small, which adds +1 to their Melee and Agility defenses and takes –1 from their Run Speed.

Hounded

The character is a member of a group of people who are persecuted for simply being themselves.

X-Gene

The character has mutant genes that cause them to develop super-powers.

Young

The character is a minor, under 18 years old. If possible, they must report in to their parents or guardians on a regular basis.
