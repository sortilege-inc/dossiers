Dossier · Triton Oracle of Keranos

Thectra Deepcaller

An exiled Triton oracle, branded heretic by Thassa's priests for the visions Keranos sends through her chest like lightning.

Stage of Career

Bioα · Vita

Thectra has no idea why Keranos gives her visions, and sometimes wishes he wouldn't — each one comes with an intense pain in her chest. Her visions upset the priests of Thassa in her home, who branded her a heretic and cast her out. She has been itinerant ever since.

She idolizes Leurybia, a former oracle of the Siren Sea who mysteriously disappeared. There is a mystical, mercurial intensity to Thectra's presence — even strangers can sense she is touched by something larger than herself.

Racial Features — Tritonη · Génos

  • Amphibious: You can breathe air and water.
  • Control Air and Water: Cast fog cloud with this trait, no slot. Gust of wind unlocks at L3, wall of water at L5. Once you cast a spell with this trait, you can't cast that spell with it again until you finish a long rest. Charisma-based.
  • Darkvision (60 ft.)
  • Emissary of the Sea: Communicate simple ideas with beasts that breathe water.
  • Guardians of the Depths: Resistance to cold damage.

Supernatural Gift — Oracleθ · Dóron

  • Ears of the Oracle: You speak, read, and write Celestial, the language of the gods. A god may deliver a message through you, in your voice or theirs, translated into any language you speak.
  • Oracle's Insight: When you make an ability check, you can roll a d10 and add the result. You can wait to decide until after seeing the d20 but before the DM declares success/failure. 1/short or long rest.
  • Oracle's Piety: Your oracle traits replace Keranos's normal piety benefits, following the Augur → Seer → Sibyl → Divine Oracle progression. Wisdom is the spellcasting ability for these traits.

Patron God — Keranos, the Storm-Eyeι · Theós

Background — Acolyteκ · Bíos

  • Skill Proficiencies: Insight, Religion.
  • Languages: Two of choice. Chosen: Sphinx, Celestial (Celestial also granted by Ears of the Oracle).
  • Equipment: Holy symbol of Keranos (a carved triton spire or sea-glass amulet), prayer wheel, 5 sticks of incense, vestments, set of common clothes, 15 gp.
  • Feature — Shelter of the Faithful: Thectra commands the respect of those who share her faith and may perform religious ceremonies of Keranos. She and her companions can expect free healing and care at a temple, shrine, or other established presence of her faith (must provide material components). Those who share her religion will support her (only her) at a modest lifestyle.

Thectra was branded heretic by Thassa's priesthood — Shelter of the Faithful applies only at temples of Keranos or other deities sympathetic to an exiled Triton oracle. Worth a GM ruling at any given site.

Why Thectra is on the Expeditionο · Hodós

Thectra's tempestuous visions may guide the party toward understanding the true stakes of the grain crisis. Her desire to defy a corrupt hierarchy could make her an enemy to those benefiting from Karametra's absence.

The Fallow Year is exactly the kind of cosmic shift Keranos has been showing her in flashes — she has every reason to seek the heart of it, and to ride the storm rather than be drowned by it.
