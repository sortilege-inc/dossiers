I.Who He Is & What He Does

Pacha is a farmer who learned to push without touching. His class is Null — a psionic discipline that dampens the power of foes through a field of mental quietude. His subclass is Metakinetic, which sharpens that quietude into kinetic edges: knockback, slide, and the sudden gathering of momentum where there should be none.

Mechanically, he is a melee combatant whose body is the weapon. His training (Density Augmentation) has given him +6 Stamina and +1 Stability already folded into his sheet. His Psionic Martial Arts let him substitute Intuition for Might on power rolls involving Knockback or Grab, and let him slide targets instead of pushing them. He moves faster than he should (Null Speed, +2 already included), and an aura around him quietly weakens whatever enemies stand close (Null Field).

Cultural Background

Culture: Secluded · Communal · Labor. He was raised apart from large settlements, in a tight-knit household, doing the work of his hands. Career: Farmer. A life of weather, livestock, and the predictable cycle of seasons — the very ordinariness of which the sheet now sits in contrast to.

III.Discipline — The Heroic Resource

Pacha's Heroic Resource is Discipline, the Null's measure of practiced stillness under pressure. The more he holds the field, the more force he has to spend.

At the start of a combat encounter (or other stressful situation tracked in combat rounds, as determined by the Director), he gains Discipline equal to his Victories. At the start of each of his turns during combat, he gains 2 Discipline. Additionally, the first time each combat round that an enemy in the area of his Null Field ability uses a main action, he gains 1 Discipline. The first time each combat round that the Director uses an ability that costs Malice, he gains 1 Discipline.

He loses any remaining Discipline at the end of the encounter.

◈ Discipline Benefits

He gains certain benefits in combat based on the amount of Discipline he has. These benefits last until the end of his turn, even if a benefit would become unavailable to him because of the amount of Discipline he spends during his turn. (See § IV. Metakinetic Mastery.)

VI.Null Field

Null Field
You project an aura that dampens the power of your foes.
Maneuver Area, Psionic 1 aura All enemies
Effect: Each target reduces their potencies by 1.
Once as a free maneuver on each of his turns, he can spend 1 Discipline and give the Null Field one of the following additional effects until the start of his next turn:
  • Gravitic Disruption: The first time on a turn that a target takes damage, he can slide them up to 2 squares.
  • Inertial Anchor: Any target who starts their turn in the area can't shift.
  • Synaptic Break: Whenever he or any ally uses an ability against a target that has a potency effect, the potency is increased by 1.
This ability remains active even after an encounter ends. It ends only if he is dying or if he willingly ends it (no action required).

VIII.Human Traits

  • Detect the Supernatural
    As a maneuver, he opens his awareness to detect supernatural creatures and phenomena. Until the end of his next turn, he knows the location of any supernatural object, or any undead, construct, or creature from another world within 5 squares, even if he doesn't have line of effect to that object or creature. He knows if he's detecting an item or a creature, and he knows the nature of any creature he detects.
  • Perseverance
    Giving up is for other people. He gains an edge on tests made using the Endurance skill. Additionally, when he is slowed, his speed is reduced to 3 instead of 2.
  • Staying Power
    His human anatomy allows him to fight, run, and stay awake longer than others. Increase his number of Recoveries by 2 (already included).

IX.Null Class Features

  • Density Augmentation
    His training has turned his body into the perfect psionic weapon, shaping pathways in his mind that enhance his physical form. He gains a +6 bonus to Stamina and a +1 bonus to stability (both already included).
  • Null Speed
    The flow of psionic power through him allows him to achieve high velocity. He gains a +2 bonus to his speed (already included).
  • Psionic Martial Arts
    Whenever he uses the Knockback or Grab maneuver, he uses Intuition instead of Might for the power roll and for determining if he can target creatures larger than him. Additionally, whenever he uses the Knockback maneuver, he can choose to slide the target instead of pushing them.
  • Monster Whisperer
    He can use the Handle Animals skill to interact with nonsapient creatures who are not animals.

XI.Equipment & Kit

The Null class fights without arms or armor in the conventional sense. Pacha's body and mind are the weapon — Density Augmentation furnishes his Stamina and Stability bonuses, and Null Speed his movement bonus. He carries no kit beyond a single annotated piece of gear.

XII.Biography

◈ Left Unwritten

Pacha's character sheet was prepared as a WPGRPGCon 2026 one-shot pre-gen and does not record an Inciting Incident, complication, age, or written backstory beyond his ancestry, culture, and career. The session has been played; per the player's instruction, this ledger does not record session events. What follows are only the threads the sheet itself sets in tension.

What the Sheet Asserts

  • He is Human. He keeps three languages — Caelian, Kalliak, Vaslorian — suggesting either travel, study, or a household in which more than one of these was spoken.
  • His culture is Secluded · Communal · Labor. He was raised away from large settlements, in a household that worked together, doing manual labor.
  • His career is Farmer. The training shows in the underlined Handle Animals skill, and in the Empathy and Read Person skills that round it out — a man whose work is reading what doesn't speak.
  • And yet: his class is Null. Somewhere in or alongside that farming life, he learned to project a field of psionic quietude and to strike faster than the eye can follow. The sheet does not say how.
