Background

Haro learned nano-sorcery in the temple of an obscure, unnamed god. Its priests and worshippers, although small in number, respect and admire his talents and potential.

Chronicle — WPGRPGCON 2026

A one-shot. An entire herd had vanished overnight. The party traced the disappearance to a hovering pyramid, and went inside.
The Bubble Servant
Haro activated an Invented Servant cypher and described the conjured thing into being: a beach-ball-sized body with smaller balls for a head and limbs — a kind of bubble boy. He sent it ahead to test whether things were safe to interact with. It worked well, for a while. Then tendrils caught it, and tore it apart. Haro was sad to see it go.
The Pressure Grenade & The Plasma Sword
Cornered by armed enemies across a long table, Haro threw a pressure grenade to the far end of the room and dove under the table for cover. The blast swept everything from the tabletop onto the floor in front of him — including, by luck, a glove cypher called a safer, which let its wearer control nearby metal. Haro slipped it on, reached out with it, and tore the metal plasma sword out of the lead enemy's hand. He turned the blade on its owner. The last one standing surrendered. The glove was given away after the fight.
