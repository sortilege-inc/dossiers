Erytheia of the Umbral Tide

Sphinx-raised, temple-foundling, white-haired and bright-eyed — a Celestial-warlock whose light is cold, and whose mercies arrive at the moment of death.

Stage of Career

Bioα · Vita

Erytheia was given to a temple of Athreos at birth — left on its steps overnight — and educated by a sphinx in the Dakra Isles. She emerged otherworldly, white-haired, bright-eyed, with a presence that other people read as touched by divine light when they're being charitable and as not entirely here when they aren't. She wants to help people toward enlightenment; she struggles with isolation, and with the persistent feeling that she is more interested in the boundary between life and death than is strictly wholesome.

Some years ago, in a brazen and desperate moment, she dared death to take her. In a way, it has. The Celestial patron she now serves makes light come out of her hands when she calls for it, but the light is cold, and what she heals tends to come back changed. She is honest about herself when she can be: she seeks power, and she judges herself harshly for it, and she judges everyone else harshly for everything else.

Feat: War Caster

  • Advantage on Constitution saves to maintain concentration on spells when you take damage.
  • You can perform the somatic components of spells even when you have weapons or a shield in one or both hands.
  • When a hostile creature's movement provokes an opportunity attack, you can use your reaction to cast a spell at it (instead of making the OA). The spell must have a casting time of 1 action and target only that creature.

Strong concentration insurance for a Cha-primary warlock — particularly once Hex, Suggestion, Mind Spike, and Hunger of the Underworld come online.

Supernatural Gift — Inscrutableθ · Dóron

Like a sphinx, you have a mind like a maze, impenetrable to mortal scrutiny.

  • Psychic Shield: Resistance to psychic damage.
  • Sphinx's Shroud: You are immune to any effect that allows other creatures to sense your emotions, read your thoughts, or determine whether you are lying.

Immune to detect thoughts, zone of truth, and similar effects. Combined with Cha-based Deception, Erytheia is functionally undetectable as a liar — a knife with no fingerprints.

Why Erytheia is on the Expeditionο · Hodós

Divine Whisperings. Erytheia initially seems a wholly good person, ministering to the poor and hungry and sick. Only slowly does it become clear that she's always around at the moments of death.

The Fallow Year creates exactly the conditions for her work: every day there are more poor and more sick and more hungry, and Athreos's skiff is busier than it has been in a long time. Erytheia would describe her work as mercy. Others might use a different word. Athreos, characteristically, doesn't mind which.
