On the Subject

Salih spends his nights charting the stars and deciphering omens for Constantinople's elite. His arcane studies have revealed patterns that quietly guide the city's fate — patrons in the Sublime Porte, the Patriarchate, and the foreign embassies all consult him, though never publicly.

He maintains a Deist's distance from any one faith, believing the cosmos itself is the only honest interlocutor. Meddling with the heavens, he knows, is never without risk; he has seen what comes of reading the stars too greedily.

Stargazer — Arcane Tradition
Omen from the Skies
Touch · 1 minute · 5 uses per long rest (Int mod) · Level 2

Touch a creature (including himself) and grant one of the following effects for 1 minute. A creature cannot have more than one Omen active. Can spend a 1st+ spell slot to regain one use (no action).

  • Bad Omen When the creature is hit, reaction to roll d10 and subtract from the attack.

  • Starry Eyes 30 ft. darkvision for 1 minute.

Familiar — Cat

A black-and-grey street cat that took to him during a night observation on a rooftop and never quite left. Per find familiar: telepathic communication within 100 ft., can deliver touch spells, can be dismissed to a pocket dimension and re-summoned with a 1-hour ritual.

Personality

Trait

I speak slowly and choose my words with care; the stars do not rush, and neither will I.

Ideal — Knowledge · Neutral

The path to understanding the cosmos is study and patience. Faith is for those who lack the time.

Bond

Years ago I read an alignment that warned of a great calamity. I told no one. It came to pass. I have not forgiven myself.

Flaw

I keep secrets from my patrons — and from my friends — because I do not trust them to handle the truths I uncover.
