I. Case Summary

Li Xia — her legal name, though she has answered to Lily Combes since her 1947 marriage — is the daughter of Chinese parents who immigrated illegally to California in 1922. She was born in the United States in January 1924 and carries citizenship by birth. Her older brother, 李勇 Li Yong, was born on the mainland and was grandfathered into citizenship following his service in the 407 Air Service Squadron.

She is, at campaign start, twenty-eight years old, married to a USAF officer currently posted to Korea, and employed as personal assistant to Dr. Raymond Kellerman at the Jet Propulsion Laboratory. She has been approached by the FBI to funnel information relating to the work of Dr. Qian Xuesen; she has agreed to cooperate on the condition that she is permitted to remain ignorant of the Bureau's intent.

She is also, at present, in the late first or early second trimester of a pregnancy resulting from a since-terminated affair. She is not yet visibly showing. Her husband does not know about the affair or the pregnancy.

Handler's Note — Entry Point

Li Xia's access vector is her position as personal assistant to Dr. Raymond Kellerman, a JPL rocket scientist who has adopted some of Jack Parsons' stranger interests. Her FBI arrangement gives her practice at compartmentalised cooperation; it does not give her any knowledge of Delta Green. Any approach must contend with the boundary she has already drawn with the Bureau: she will cooperate if she is permitted to not know.

II. Background

Family

Her parents entered California illegally in 1922. Her older brother Li Yong was born on the mainland and his citizenship was secured through his service in the 407 Air Service Squadron. Li Xia herself was born in the United States in 1924, full citizen by right of birth.

Wartime Service

She served briefly as a translator during the Second World War before enlisting in the Madame Chiang Kai-shek Air WAC in 1943 at the age of nineteen. There she learned to review and interpret aerial surveillance photographs — the analytical skill that has shaped every subsequent posting. She served in the Philippines, where she met Jeremy Combes, a USAAF officer.

Marriage and Education

After the war, she and Jeremy moved to Hawai'i together. They were married in Honolulu in early 1947. She enrolled at the University of Hawai'i and completed a Bachelor's degree in East Asian languages. Toward the end of her studies, Jeremy began to spend more time in California, taking on a role as USAF liaison with the Jet Propulsion Laboratory on the subjects of experimental aircraft and future air strategies.

California and JPL

On graduation in 1949, Lily followed Jeremy to California. Through her WAC connections she found work as a research assistant at the Space and Missile Systems Center, contributing to LORAN station synchronisation improvements. When the Korean War began and Jeremy was deployed to Korea, he coordinated a secretarial position for her at the JPL itself.

She made a name for herself quickly through a combination of aggressive networking and stern soft power, running the administrative side of the laboratory. Within a year, her friendship with Jiang Ying — wife of Dr. Qian Xuesen — led to her promotion to personal assistant to Dr. Raymond Kellerman, a rocket scientist who has taken on some of Jack Parsons' stranger interests.

Compartmentalised — Personal

An affair, since terminated, has resulted in a pregnancy that was not. She is in the late first or early second trimester at campaign start, not yet showing. Jeremy is in Korea and does not know.

III. Current Situation

The FBI Arrangement

The Bureau has approached her to funnel information relating to Qian Xuesen's work. The terms she set are explicit: she will cooperate if she is permitted to remain ignorant of what the Bureau intends to do with what she provides. This is the load-bearing structure of her current participation. It is also structurally fragile — the arrangement depends on no one telling her what it is for.

Dr. Kellerman

Her direct principal at JPL. A rocket scientist who has adopted some of Jack Parsons' stranger interests. The specifics of those interests, and the extent to which Lily has encountered them in her work, are not documented in this file.

Operational Concern

The Bureau's interest in Xuesen and Delta Green's interest in Kellerman are not the same investigation. Lily has agreed to participate in one of them. She has not agreed to participate in two.
