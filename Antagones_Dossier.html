Dossier & Circumstances

Antagones rose from the sun-scorched training pits to become the arena's most electrifying champion — a Leonin warrior whose roar alone sent crowds into frenzied adulation. Three seasons of undefeated bouts. Sellout crowds. Merchandise hawked in every alley of the city. He was not merely a gladiator; he was an institution.

He is also, regrettably, catastrophically naive about money. The management of the Crimson Coliseum — recognising that Antagones' contract renewal would cost them dearly — convened in private session. They concluded it was cheaper to manufacture a problem than pay his worth.

They told him he was "slipping." They cited fabricated reports of slower reflexes, reduced crowd enthusiasm, whisper campaigns of younger rivals eclipsing him. They offered a "voluntary sabbatical" — framed as compassionate rest — while the arena planned his triumphant return: a carefully orchestrated comeback tour that would make them triple what they owed him in back pay, with Antagones none the wiser.

He believed every word. His Wisdom score is, after all, a 6. Armed with a burning need to prove himself and a jaw set in wounded pride, he gathered his considerable arsenal and went adventuring — absolutely convinced that he must become worthy again. He sends back letters describing his exploits in excruciating detail, addressed to management, who read them aloud at drinking parties.

Current whereabouts: Unknown. Last confirmed sighting: heading north toward the Thornback Wastes wielding a Trident of Paralysis and asking locals if they had heard of him.

Character Profile
Personality
"I will settle for nothing less than perfection." He means it. He practices his victory poses daily, alone, in whatever cave or barn he currently occupies.
Ideal
Tradition. The legends of past champions must never be forgotten — for they teach us what we owe the crowd. He is particularly devoted to Iroas and views every fight as an offering.
Bond
"I will do anything to prove myself superior to my hated rival." He does not have a specific rival. Management invented one. He has nevertheless committed fully to defeating this fictional person.
Flaw
He cannot keep his true feelings hidden. His sharp tongue lands him in trouble constantly — and yet, paradoxically, his WIS of 6 means he rarely sees the trouble coming until he is already in it.
Key Abilities & Features
Daunting Roar (Leonin)
Bonus action. Creatures of his choice within 10 ft. must make a DC 17 WIS save or be frightened until end of next turn. Witnesses describe this as deeply upsetting.
Divine Smite (Paladin)
Expends spell slots to add 2d8–6d8 radiant damage to melee strikes. Currently has 4× 1st-level and 3× 2nd-level slots remaining. Uses them liberally.
Action Surge (Fighter)
Once per rest, takes an additional action on his turn. Combined with Extra Attack, this is an unconscionable number of things happening at once.
Aura of Protection (Paladin)
Allies within 10 ft. gain +3 to all saving throws. He is genuinely excellent at protecting others. He is simply unable to protect himself from a confident lie told to his face.
Sentinel (Feat)
Enemies cannot escape his reach unpunished. Opportunity attacks reduce speed to 0. He cannot disengage from a fight; this is also true emotionally.
By Popular Demand (Background)
At any combat venue, he can perform for free lodging and food. He has activated this in every village he's passed through. He is, wherever he goes, inexplicably famous already.
