Dossier & Circumstances

Antagones was a gladiator celebrity — popular, profitable, and (by his own assessment) the finest performer his arena had to offer. He maintains, to anyone who will listen, that he is the best gladiator in town. Refunds offered if proven otherwise.

Then management told him he was slipping. They sat him down, explained gently that he wasn't what he used to be, and benched him for a season to rest and recover. They would, of course, welcome him back when he was ready.

None of this was true. Management simply did not want to keep paying him, and intended to bring him back later to enormous fanfare — and enormous profit — once they had wrung a season's savings out of his absence. Antagones, whose Wisdom score is a 6, believed every word.

And so, wounded in pride and burning to prove himself worthy again, he gathered his arsenal and set out adventuring. He was, after all, a gladiator. He would simply have to demonstrate — through deeds of impossible glory in distant lands — that he had never been slipping at all.

Key Features & Traits
Daunting Roar (Leonin)
Bonus action. Creatures of his choice within 10 ft. must make a DC 17 Wisdom save or be frightened until the end of his next turn. Recharges on a short or long rest.
Claws (Leonin)
Natural weapons. Unarmed strikes deal 1d4 + STR slashing damage instead of bludgeoning.
Darkvision (Leonin)
Sees in dim light within 60 ft. as if bright, and in darkness as if dim (greyscale only).
Hunter's Instincts (Leonin)
Proficiency in Intimidation, granted by lineage.
Divine Smite (Paladin)
Expend a spell slot on a melee hit for 2d8 radiant damage (1st level), +1d8 per slot level above 1st, max 5d8. +1d8 against fiends and undead.
Improved Divine Smite (Paladin)
All of his melee weapon hits deal an additional 1d8 radiant damage. Slots not required.
Lay on Hands (Paladin)
Pool of 35 hit points to restore by touch. May expend 5 HP from the pool to cure one disease or neutralize one poison.
Aura of Protection (Paladin 6)
He and allies within 10 ft. add +3 (his CHA modifier) to all saving throws while he is conscious.
Divine Sense (Paladin)
As an action, detect celestials, fiends, and undead within 60 ft. 4 uses per long rest.
Divine Health (Paladin)
Immune to disease.
Action Surge (Fighter)
Once per short or long rest, take one additional action on his turn.
Second Wind (Fighter)
Bonus action: regain 1d10 + 4 hit points. Recharges on a short or long rest.
Extra Attack
Attack twice instead of once when taking the Attack action.
Sentinel (Feat)
Opportunity attack hits reduce target speed to 0. Disengaging foes still provoke. May react to attack creatures within 5 ft. that strike others.
Charger (Feat)
When Dashing, may use a bonus action to make a melee attack or shove. +5 damage or 10 ft. push after moving 10 ft. straight.
Dual Wielder (Feat)
+1 AC with two melee weapons. Two-weapon fighting works with non-light weapons. May draw or stow two weapons at once.
Martial Adept (Feat)
Two Battle Master maneuvers and one d6 superiority die, regained on a short or long rest.
Piercer (Feat)
On piercing attacks: reroll one damage die once per turn; extra damage die on piercing criticals.
Piety to Iroas
Devoted to the god of victory. Piety grows through great victories, honourable conduct, single combat, and feats of skill. Diminished by cowardice, deceit against honourable foes, or harm to innocents.
By Popular Demand (Background)
May always find a place to perform at any venue featuring combat for entertainment, earning modest or comfortable lodging and food. Locals who recognize him from prior performances tend to take a liking to him.
