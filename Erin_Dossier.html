Order of the Vance · 1st-Degree Postulant

When we want to use a spell, we read the complex formulae, lengthy linguistic keys, intricate images, and elaborate instructions and draw it all into ourselves, preparing it to be cast later. The uninitiated call this memorization, but it is more an act of absorption or consumption. Some of us call it clothing ourselves in our spells — each one is like putting on an additional garment.

Each spell takes up virtual space within us — a 3″×3″ square at 1st degree. Alpha-class spells occupy 3″×1.5″; beta-class, 3″×3″. Preparation takes about an hour regardless of the number prepared. Casting requires only an action, but retaining a spell after casting costs Sorcery equal to its level.

Authority & Responsibilities

A 1st-degree Vance has authority over journeymen — small services, casual favors, errands, messages, filings. Required to perform similar duties for those of higher degree.

Vance House Rules

Vances get a cost-reduction on metamagic secrets. Divide the cost by their rank; they can be applied for free on vance-spells that are released.

Forte · Brandishes Battlemagic

Spells kill. Incantations destroy. A master at using spells that kill and keep others from killing me. A killer. Battlemagic practices are sometimes called murderspells, and rightfully so. Sometimes a soldier, sometimes an assassin for hire — compassion or hesitation are luxuries for other people.

Battlemagic Armorsuit

Invisible · Level 1 · No Cost

I can make and safely wear a Battlemagic Armorsuit and gain its benefits. The suit grows more protective the longer I wear it. At 1–2 forte abilities: +1 Armor, +1 Resist. If anyone other than its creator wears it, the suit inflicts 1 point of damage each round and confers no benefits.

Path to Joy · Path to Despair

To Joy

  • Learn a spell of a level higher than any we have yet learned.
  • Attend the Conclave of Iov for the first time.
  • Create a new Vancian spell (and name it after yourself).
  • Learn a spell from the Vancian who created it (assuming they outrank you).
  • Spend a month instructing lower-ranking Vancians.
  • Kill a higher-level foe than I have ever killed with my murderspells.
  • Solve a problem through violence where others would have tried a different tactic.

To Despair

  • Chastisement from a higher-ranking Vancian.
  • A spell producing a different effect than expected (not failure — simply something else).
  • Complete separation from the order and any of its members for more than three months.
  • I am bested in battle by magic.
  • I face a challenge that cannot be solved through violence.

Character Arc · Repay A Debt

You owe someone something, and it is time to make good.

OPENING ✦
Debts Come Due — 1 Acumen. You determine to do what is needed to make good on the debt. It may involve repaying money, but more appropriately it is performing a deed or a series of deeds.
STEP
Talking It Over — 1 Acumen. You discuss the matter with the person you owe, if possible. You ensure that what you're doing is what they want.
CLIMAX
Repayment — 3 Acumen. Earn the money or goods, or undertake a major task. Success: 1 Joy. Failure: 1 Despair.
RESOLUTION
1 Acumen. You relax knowing your debt is repaid, and you look to the future.

Field Log

Session I · A Gothic Tower in Fartown
A student's paper, a thread of green-sun bleed, and the trail leads to a 9-story gothic tower. I kicked in the door and dropped Grudge around the three other intruders — a professor and two students — then cast Vision of the Nemesis. A glowing female statue, leaking moss and fungi, gaining power, whispered "set me free…" Then the cracks healed and the objects faded from life. +1 Acumen. The questions remain.
Session II · A Hand Bearing an Invitation
At Obliteration, where Constantin is now researching on my behalf in exchange for bar tab, a hand floated into the bar carrying a sealed, perfumed envelope marked ENT. It came to the attention of Velvet Thatcher — 3rd-Degree Vance, politically ambitious, aiming for the Council of Iov — that I had performed a great service to the Vancian Campus. I am invited to an exclusive party this Friday at The Cut Rug, a dance hall in the Palindrome. I RSVPed by coaster, with a +1. Constantin received his own invitation; so did Veridian, hers from Victor.
