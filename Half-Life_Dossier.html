History

Cody went to jail for assaulting a school resource officer during an altercation at his high school in Atlanta. Tried and convicted as an adult.

Assault on a law enforcement officer. Tried as adult. Conviction on file. Currently in violation of parole conditions. Has not been outed as a mutant — so far.

Joined X-FRONT following release. The mutant angle remains hidden from the state. Whether that lasts is another question.

Psych Profile

Personality
Aggressive, unfocused, impulsive. Deep resentment of the state and its machinery. Runs on opposition rather than direction.
Goal
Seeking to shake off the restrictions and constraints that have defined his life. Freedom from, not freedom for.
Barrier
In denial that "freedom from" is sufficient. His goal is purely reductive — he lacks any positive vision of the future, for himself or for others.
