Class & Subclass Features
Umbral Corruption Campaign
As you gain scars, you become slowly corrupted by the Umbra. When you deal damage, you deal extra damage equal to your number of scars. When you mark your last Hope slot with a scar, you don't retire — you instead succumb to the Umbra's influence and charge forever into the dark to join the terrors that stalk lightless spaces.
Volatile Magic Hope Feature
Spend 3 Hope to reroll any number of your damage dice on an attack that deals magic damage.
Arcane Sense Sorcerer
You can sense the presence of magical people and objects within Close range.
Minor Illusion Sorcerer
Make a Spellcast Roll (10). On a success, create a minor visual illusion no larger than yourself within Close range. The illusion is convincing to anyone at Close range or farther.
Channel Raw Power Sorcerer
Once per long rest, place a domain card from your loadout into your vault and choose one:
  • Gain Hope equal to the level of the card.
  • Enhance a spell that deals damage, gaining a bonus to your damage roll equal to twice the level of the card.
Manipulate Magic Primal Origin · Foundation
Your primal origin allows you to modify the essence of magic itself. After you cast a spell or make an attack using a weapon that deals magic damage, you can mark a Stress to do one of the following:
  • Extend the spell or attack's reach by one range
  • Gain a +2 bonus to the action roll's result
  • Double a damage die of your choice
  • Hit an additional target within range
Found Family Community · Reborne
Once per session, you can spend a Hope to use an ally's community ability. When you do, your ally gains a Hope. At any point, when you've discovered the community you were once a part of — or have joined a new community — you can permanently trade this community card for that one instead.

Character

Origin

A stone figure recovered from the mountainside, reactivated after decades of silence. Painted with white ochre runes. Their voice is soft and bell-like. They are slow-moving and deeply thoughtful, and they revere the Sacred Pyre.

Companion to Ygva

Kelun has been Ygva's confidant since her childhood. She would play echo games with them in the snow and confide her fears to them. When the elders did not permit Kelun to join Renavir's lessons, Ygva recounted everything to them each night — not just what Renavir taught, but how he said it.

Five Years on the Ledge

When Oshel came down from her ledge and pointed at Ygva, Vaetra urged Ygva to take Kelun with her. Kelun accompanied Ygva into the witch's isolation and lived alongside her under Oshel's tutelage for five years. They returned with Ygva to Ulmira's Step, and then to the mountains above the settlement.

Of Late

Kelun has been learning, and seems able to master some rudimentary magic.
