Physical Appearance 外見

A small Tenebrate girl with violet skin and bright, dark-pupiled eyes set in a soft, picture-book face. Her hair is short and periwinkle-blue, cut just past the jaw, pointed ears showing through. A great pair of warm-brown antlers branches above her head — her Dark Gift, sized for someone three times her height, worn without apology.

She wears questing plate of dull steel over a tunic of deep teal, with a long mustard-gold scarf wound at her throat and trailing behind her like a banner. A round wooden shield rides at her back. The armor is fitted but heavy; she stands with feet planted, knees bent, in the brace of someone used to recoil.

In her hands, the dissonance: a Goblin Belcher of riveted iron and stained wood, its muzzle hammered into the leering, fanged maw of a goblin's head. The barrel is taller than her torso. She smiles softly while holding it, the way a child might smile while holding a favourite toy.
