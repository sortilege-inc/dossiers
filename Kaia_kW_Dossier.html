I. Identity & Standing

Kaia kW — born Kai — is a trans Pacific Islander Rockerboy, vocalist of the Night City band Homeschool Dropouts, and a recent addition to the edgerunner crew known as the Preem Chooms. She arrived in Night City with her brother Mikey after the sinking of the Arasaka Ryūjin, the Pacific supercontainer-ship trading megacomplex where she grew up. Session record describes her in-fiction as an experimental noise musician; her player's setting notes describe the band as hardcore punk with metallic elements. Both descriptions stand on this file.

Physical Description

Hair short and curly (lifepath); nose piercings (lifepath); gang colors of the SNCZ worn as a full kit — bottoms, top, footwear, jewelry, and contact lenses (inventory). She wears a Laser Light Street Jacket (SP 11), which counts as Light Tattoo in the Urban Flash style. BODY 4 is a small frame; her precise age is not recorded.

Manner of Presence

Lifepath personality: moody, rash, headstrong. About people: hates almost everyone. Most-valued person on the lifepath: yourself. Most-valued possession: family photograph. What she values: knowing state of the scene, who's who.

Tenure

Her tenure as vocalist is not given as an exact figure in the character record. The record describes a sequence of departures and recruitments — "a year in or so" the first wave hit, then several further rounds — consistent with her being the longest-tenured member of the current lineup by a meaningful margin. The player's working estimate is roughly four years; that figure is consistent with the record but is not in itself canonical.

Sound & Performance

Per Session 1, the crew describes Kaia in-fiction as "the experimental noise musician." Per her player's setting notes, the band's working sound is hardcore punk with metallic elements, with screamed/shouted vocals processed through her AudioVox. The two descriptions live side by side on this file rather than one displacing the other. Her on-stage equipment includes the KillStrom Sonic Boom Amp, which, when linked to her instrument, can force people in the area to make a Resist Torture/Drugs check.

The Distraction Gig (Sessions 2–3)

For the Biotechnica job, Kaia produced a concert poster titled "Harusaka" and arranged a pop-up show across from the target building, co-billed with Candy Unicorn under Candy's manager Bluejack's conditions. She also cut a rough music video for the gig. She finished her set before joining the rest of the crew for the infiltration.

VIII. Open Threads & Antagonists

The Critic / Influencer

The character record states, in the GM-notes field, that a critic/influencer is gunning for her. No name is given. The lifepath enemies field reads "None as yet," which the dossier reads as the lifepath line not yet having been formalised into a named antagonist on the sheet. The critic/influencer is treated here as a real, active, unnamed antagonist. They have not yet been introduced on-screen.

Mikey

Her brother is somewhere in Night City and has not replied to a message in months (GM notes). Not yet surfaced in play.

The Sniper at the Exfil

During the Combat Zone exfil (Session 4) a sniper hit Shark. Mr. Purvis denied involvement. The shooter has not been identified.

The Red Chrome Legion

Stormed the Lily Pond looking for Candy Unicorn in Session 1. Kaia used the venue's strobe lighting against them, threw a flashbang, and killed one of them point-blank. Candy speculated the attack was retaliation for her past actions against the Legion. Whether the Legion has identified the people who fought alongside Candy that night is unresolved.

The Maelstrom Tag

The "Maelstrom choom" credit on Kaia's reputation ledger is a sticker some audiences may read very differently from others. Boomer and Dozer are not enemies; the question is who else now knows their faces and hers are on the same incident.

The Jessica Donuts Booking

A substantial offer to book Homeschool Dropouts for a corporate-adjacent birthday. Deka has promised to arrange it ("Night City Connections"). Kaia has not yet met Jessica and has not yet committed.

After a break-in, Kaia is found asleep in the Preem Chooms' vehicle with her amplifier. Cain approaches with a shotgun; the encounter de-escalates into a conversation about her finances. The crew helps her move the amplifier to her apartment.

The Lily Pond — Red Chrome Legion attack
McKracken draws roughly a dozen fans for Kaia in Old Japantown. She connects with Candy Unicorn. Masked Red Chrome Legion members storm the bar looking for Candy. Kaia uses the strobe lighting to disorient them, throws a flashbang, and finishes one of them at point-blank range. A bouncer is killed in the firefight; the patrons scatter.
// Session 2 · The Delirium
Mr. Purvis · the Biotechnica contract
At The Delirium, Purvis hires the crew to eliminate Emerald City and deliver 30 kg. 1,500 eb up front, 1,000 eb on completion; C9 and detonator provided. Kaia volunteers for demolitions training. She designs the "Harusaka" concert poster and coordinates the pop-up gig with Candy Unicorn under Bluejack's conditions. Cuts a rough music video. C9 and detonator stored at Candy's studio.
// Sessions 3–4 · The Old Combat Zone
The job · the basement · the collapse
Kaia and Candy run the pop-up concert as cover; Kaia finishes her set before joining the rest of the crew at the propped-open back door. In the basement greenhouse-and-vat farm, the crew encounter Scarecrow gangers. Kaia kills one with a point-blank shotgun blast and attaches the C9 to the central vat. The C9 detonates prematurely; the building collapses; Kaia and Deka are buried under rubble. Cain and McKracken extract them while a sniper hits Shark. The crew reach a hospital.
// Session 4 · aftermath
Payout · rebuff at the Blackout Bar · debt cleared
Purvis pays out the 1,000 eb on completion; the suitcase is not trapped. Kaia tries to get a new gig at the Blackout Bar and is rebuffed. She pays off the 550 eb she owed Lou at the Blackout Bar (wealth ledger). Cain prepares for the Nevada run; Kaia crashes at McKracken's place.
// Jan 14 · "Night City Connections"
Throat implant consult · covers Cain's rent
Lennox Conway, a Trauma Team medic, is brought in by Shark to consult on the cybernetic throat implant. Shark agrees to help find a surgeon on a favours-for-work basis. Kaia agrees to cover Cain's rent at McKracken's for the back half of January (due by the 31st). Candy Unicorn shares jam-session clips on GardenBook.
// "Night City Connections"
The broken van · the Maelstrom joyride · the river
Kaia tries to pick the mechanical lock on a broken van in the Combat Zone. Two Maelstrom gangers — Boomer and Dozer — appear and invite her on a joyride shooting at NCPD. A ricochet punctures a rear tire; the van goes off a bridge into the river. Kaia escapes through the passenger side. Boomer and Dozer surface, flag down a passing boat, and flip off the NCPD. Street Rep ticks to 3 on the "Maelstrom choom" credit.
// "Night City Connections" · that evening
Speedheal · Mort's karaoke
Lennox administers Speedheal — first to Deka, then a second dose to Kaia, lifting her serious-wound penalties. Kaia invites Lennox, Shark, and McKracken to Mort's, a dive bar known for karaoke. McKracken performs inebriated; Kaia impresses a fan who buys drinks for the table.
// "Night City Connections" · current
The Jessica Donuts booking
Deka's date with Jessica Donuts goes well. Jessica is eager to book Homeschool Dropouts for her birthday — a substantial performance, per Deka. Deka promises to arrange it. The booking is pending.
