I. Overview

Tig is a 14th-level Variant Human Fighter of the Battle Master archetype, built around high-DEX dual scimitar combat and tactical superiority-dice usage. The City Watch / Investigator background situates him as a former law enforcer with an investigator's disposition. Languages spoken: Common, Abyssal, Elvish, Halfling, Undercommon.

V. Weapons & Equipment

Weapons — Equipped

Scimitar (×2, Dual Wielded)
Attack +9
Damage 1d6 + 4 slashing
Properties Finesse, Light
Mastery Nick
Weight 3 lb each
Attack roll uses DEX (+4) + proficiency (+5) = +9. Both scimitars are equipped simultaneously and benefit from the Dual Wielder feat (+1 AC while wielding two melee weapons). The Nick mastery property means the extra Light-weapon attack granted by Two-Weapon Fighting can be made as part of the Attack action rather than a Bonus Action — effectively freeing Tig's Bonus Action. With Extra Attack (3 attacks at level 11) plus Nick, Tig can deliver four scimitar strikes in a single Attack action turn. Slasher feat applies to these attacks: on a hit, target speed reduced by 10 ft; on a critical hit, target has Disadvantage on all attacks until start of Tig's next turn.

VI. Class Features

Core Fighter Features (Level 14)

FeatureDescription
Extra Attack (×3) When taking the Attack action, Tig attacks three times. Combined with the Nick mastery property on dual scimitars, this yields four effective strikes per Attack action.
Action Surge Once per short or long rest, take one additional action on a turn. At level 17 this becomes twice per rest; at level 14 it is still once. Can be used to double the total attack output.
Second Wind Bonus action once per short or long rest: regain 1d10 + 14 hit points.
Indomitable (×2) Reroll a failed saving throw, keeping the new result. Available twice between long rests (unlocked at level 13). Once per long rest at levels 9–12; twice from level 13 onward.
Fighting Style Two Fighting Styles selected at levels 1 and 6 (plus one from the Fighting Initiate feat). Specific styles not recorded on the sheet — see Campaign Notes.

Battle Master Archetype (Level 14)

FeatureDescription
Superiority Dice 6d10 per short or long rest. At level 14 the Battle Master has 6 superiority dice, each a d10 (upgraded from d8 at level 10). Spent to fuel Combat Maneuvers. Save DC for opposed maneuvers: DC 17 (8 + proficiency +5 + STR +1 or DEX +4; if DEX-based maneuver, DC = 8 + 5 + 4 = 17).
Combat Maneuvers (6 known) Six maneuvers selected from the Battle Master list. The specific maneuvers chosen are not recorded on the exported sheet — see Campaign Notes.
Know Your Enemy After spending at least 1 minute observing or interacting with a creature outside of combat, learn how it compares to Tig in two of the following: STR, DEX, CON, AC, current HP, class levels (if any), Fighter levels (if any).
Relentless If Tig has no Superiority Dice remaining at the start of his turn, he regains one. (Level 15 feature — listed here because the sheet reflects level 14; this feature unlocks at 15.)

VII. Feats

A level-14 Fighter receives ASI/feat slots at levels 4, 6, 8, 12, and 14, plus one from the Variant Human racial trait — six total slots. The five feats below account for five of those six slots. The sixth slot's use (ASI or additional feat) is not recorded on the sheet.

FeatSourceEffect
Defensive Duelist ASI/Feat slot Prerequisite: DEX 13+. Reaction: when wielding a finesse weapon and a creature hits Tig with a melee attack, add proficiency bonus (+5) to AC for that attack, potentially turning a hit into a miss.
Dual Wielder ASI/Feat slot +1 bonus to AC while wielding a separate melee weapon in each hand. Can use Two-Weapon Fighting with non-Light weapons. Can draw or stow two one-handed weapons per turn instead of one.
Prodigy ASI/Feat slot Human/Half-Elf/Half-Orc only. Grants: one skill proficiency (not recorded), one tool proficiency (not recorded), one language proficiency (not recorded), and expertise in one already-proficient skill. Acrobatics has double proficiency (+14), marking it as the expertise choice.
Fighting Initiate ASI/Feat slot Prerequisite: proficiency with a martial weapon. Grants one additional Fighting Style from the Fighter list. Specific style not recorded — see Campaign Notes.
Slasher ASI/Feat slot +1 to STR or DEX (already factored into scores). Once per turn on a slashing damage hit: reduce target's speed by 10 ft until start of Tig's next turn. On a critical hit dealing slashing damage: target has Disadvantage on all attack rolls until start of Tig's next turn.

VIII. Background & Character

Background: City Watch / Investigator (Variant)

Background Feature — Watcher's Eye
Experience enforcing the law gives Tig an instinct for local power structures. He can easily locate the nearest watch outpost or law-enforcement body in any settlement, and can just as readily identify the centres of criminal activity — though he is more welcome in one than the other.

Background skill proficiencies: Athletics, Insight (Investigator variant substitutes Investigation for Athletics; both are listed as proficient on this sheet, suggesting the Investigator variant was taken and Athletics was separately acquired). Background languages: two of choice (not specified beyond those already listed).

Starting equipment from background: a watch uniform indicating rank, a horn for summoning assistance, a set of manacles, and 10 gp.
