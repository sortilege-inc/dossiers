Thalassa Wavebinder

A Triton evoker of the deep, whose interest in the slow secrets of the ocean and the slow turning of the heavens are, to her, two faces of the same study.

Stage of Career

Bioα · Vita

Why wouldn't Thalassa phrase every statement as a question? It is one of the small habits that mark her as touched by something other than herself — an inscrutability she has carried since childhood. She is a researcher of evocation magic, devoted to Thassa, and her interest in the slow secrets of the deep ocean and the slow turning of the heavens are, to her, two faces of the same study.

She dreamed once of a great kraken; when she followed the dream to the sea, the kraken appeared to her and gave her Thassa's blessing. She has been a devotee of the goddess of the deep ever since.

    Why Thalassa is on the Expeditionο · Hodós

    Thalassa's evocation expertise might allow her to uncover ancient weather or agricultural spells tied to Karametra's domain. As an astronomer, she might also interpret signs in the heavens pointing to the root cause of the divine absence.

Thalassa would not have chosen this expedition — her caution would warn her against it. But once the questions are asked — why has Karametra fallen silent, what do the stars say — she cannot stop her own curiosity from following.
