History

Tabitha briefly attended Xavier's Academy and concluded it was cowardly at best. After repeated arguments with the teaching staff, she dropped out.

Felt the school's approach amounted to accommodation. The arguments weren't just philosophical — they were loud, escalating, and final. She didn't leave; she burned it on her way out.

Met Toya at a mutant pride march that went south. When the crowd got rough and the situation turned dangerous, the two of them found each other on the same side of the line — and X-FRONT grew from there.

Psych Profile

Personality
Full of spirit and self-sure. Runs hot. Doesn't do uncertainty quietly.
Goal
Doesn't see a future worth having for herself — only as a means to the end of other mutants having one. She's going to fight hard and go down fighting.
Barrier
Has broken off from everyone who felt limiting — which stripped her of institutional and social support. The future she wants to build, she can't build alone, but she almost compulsively burns every bridge she crosses.
