History

They briefly attended Xavier's academy and felt that it was cowardly at best. After repeated arguments and conflicts with the teachers, they dropped out.

Met Toya at a mutant pride march that went south, and formed the seeds of X-FRONT together.

Psych Profile

Personality
Full of spirit and self-sure.
Goal
Tabitha doesn't see a future for herself worth having, except if it's a means to the end of other mutants also having a future. She's going to fight hard and go down fighting.
Barrier
Tabitha has broken off from those she felt limited her, but that also deprives her of institutional and social support. The future she wants to build, she can't build alone, but she almost compulsively burns every bridge she crosses.

Powers

Combat Trickery
Reaction · Instant · Cost: 5 Focus
Once per battle, when the character is attacking targets of equal or higher rank, the character automatically rolls a 1 on their Marvel die, and that die cannot be affected by trouble. If the character is attacking multiple targets, all the targets must be of equal or higher rank.
Inspiration
Standard · 1 Round
The character inspires an ally in earshot. The ally gains an edge on all action checks until the start of the character's next turn.
Uncanny
Permanent
Any damage multiplier for attacks against the character's Focus is reduced by 1.
◈ Luck
Jinx You
Standard · Concentration · 5 Focus/turn · Reach
The character makes a Melee check to touch the target. On a success, the power takes effect, and that foe has trouble on all action checks made for the power's duration. On a Fantastic success, the target also takes standard (not doubled) Melee damage.
Evil Eye
Standard · Concentration · 5 Focus/turn · 10 spaces
The character picks a foe in their line of sight. That foe has trouble on all action checks made for the power's duration. There is no limit on the range between the character and the foe once the power is activated.
Lucky Me
Standard · Concentration · 5 Focus/turn
The character gains an edge on all action checks made while this power is in effect. The character must pay the Focus cost at the start of each of their subsequent turns of concentration to keep the power working.
Lucky You
Standard · Concentration · 5 Focus/turn · Reach
The character touches an ally. That ally gains an edge on all action checks made while this power is in effect.
Wish You Luck
Standard · Concentration · 5 Focus/turn · 10 spaces
The character picks an ally in line of sight. That ally gains an edge on all action checks made while this power is in effect. There is no limit on the range between the character and the ally once the power is activated.

Traits & Tags

Battle Ready

The character is always mentally prepared for any sort of trouble to start. Add +30 Focus.

Connections (Mutants)

By making an Ego check, the character can call on their contact to provide help in the form of clues, information or resources. The Narrator determines the TN of the Ego check based on the favor requested.

Fearless

The character is extremely brave. They have an edge on any action checks required to deal with fear.

Public Speaking

The character knows how to get the attention of a crowd, whether that's at a political rally or in front of a jury or a classroom. They have an edge on Ego checks when attempting to persuade groups.

Black Market Access

The character knows how and where they can buy and sell hard-to-find and potentially illegal things.

Hounded

The character is a member of a group of people who are persecuted for simply being themselves.

X-Gene

The character has mutant genes that cause them to develop super-powers.

Young

The character is a minor, under 18 years old. If possible, they must report in to their parents or guardians on a regular basis.
