Miele Nyx-Sighted

A Satyr who sees Nyx where others see only night — a psionic Talent destined, she suspects, to uncover the secret of the gods' power.

Stage of Career

Bioα · Vita

Miele is a Satyr with the sight — that is, she sees Nyx where others see only night. Her psionic awakening came at the Academy of Creation, and what she saw on her first manifestation has not left her: an outline of a god whose name was not in any of the catalogs the Academy keeps.

She cheats at games of chance. She jokes at inappropriate times. She is — and this is a thing the satyrs of her revel will tell you with rueful affection — insufferable at parties because she is always trying to tell you the secret you don't yet realize you need to know. Kruphix has noticed. Kruphix has not yet decided what to do about it.

She believes she is destined to uncover the secret of the gods' power. She has not told anyone this. She is not entirely sure whether this is a true belief or one that has been planted in her by something that doesn't want her to look elsewhere.
