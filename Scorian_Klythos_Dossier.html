Bioα · Vita

Scorian was born the moment Mount Velus erupted — a sign, his elders said, of Purphoros's blessing on his arrival. The molten rock he now uses as an arcane focus formed in that hour, though Scorian himself did not pick it up until much later, the day he first harnessed Wild Magic and felt the fire under his skin make sense at last.

He faces problems head-on. A simple, direct solution is the best path. The chaos inside him is a flame to be tended, not extinguished — and he is sure that there is an enemy out there, somewhere, set against him.

Why Scorian is on the Expeditionο · Hodós

Following the Heat. The Fallow Year reads, to an Oread, as Karametra ceding what is hers. Purphoros's fire and the slow turning of seasons are old rivals — and a long winter strengthens whoever still has heat to give. Scorian would not call this opportunity, but he would not call it tragedy either.

He follows his passion, and his passion has begun whispering that the answer to Theros's grief lies somewhere mortals will need to walk into hot, dangerous places. The expedition is going there.
