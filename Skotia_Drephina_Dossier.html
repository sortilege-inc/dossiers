Skotia Drephina

A Lampad nymph who walks the shadowed paths between worlds, and who answers to no god — though several have noticed her.

Stage of Career

Bioα · Vita

Skotia walks the shadowed paths between worlds — a Lampad, the rarest sort of nymph, born to wander caves and graveyards and the dim approaches to the Underworld. She believes that multiple gods seek to use her to their ends, and she works hard to avoid all of their entanglements. The shadows are her refuge, and no one — divine or mortal — can control where she walks or what she sees.

Her ethereal-white eyes glow faintly even in daylight; her hair is a flowing mist that shifts in texture; her skin holds a dusky purple sparkle. Most who see her assume she is a ghost. She doesn't disabuse them.

Why Skotia is on the Expeditionο · Hodós

Skotia normally avoids divine entanglements — but the Fallow Year is divine entanglement on a continental scale, and her customary "stay in the shadows" strategy will eventually fail as the famine wears on.

There is also personal reason. Lampads are caretakers of the path from life to the Underworld, and a famine on Karametra's scale means the Tartyx River will swell with souls she might rather see live. Her position lets her gather intelligence others can't reach, and she will be asked to do so. Whether she calls that duty or pragmatism is her own affair.
