Bioα · Vita

Zephyrus is a famous runner, known across Meletan lands as "Zephyrus of Sitrium" for a record-setting journey on foot from his home village to the polis of Meletis itself. He runs because he believes the gods want him dead, and intends to stay one step ahead of them — physically as much as spiritually.

He has studied with a sphinx (in company with Erytheia of the Umbral Tide) and with a druid (in company with Alesta Centaur-Speaker).
